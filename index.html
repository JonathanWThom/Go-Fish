Simpsons Go Fish

Game Rules:
  • Each player gets five cards. If you are dealt a four of a kind, or get four of a kind during game play, those cards are turned into a book.
  • When it’s your turn, ask Homer for a rank of card that you need (e.g. 2, 7, King, etc.). This must be a card in your hand. If Homer has the card you want, he must pass it over. If not, you must "Go Fish" and draw a card from the deck.
  • Then, Homer will ask you for a rank of card. If you have it, you must fork it over. If not, Homer will "Go Fish!"
  • If either player has to "Go Fish," but the deck is empty, it becomes the other player's turn.
  • The game ends when one player wins by compiling 7 books.
